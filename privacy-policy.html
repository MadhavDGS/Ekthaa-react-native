Privacy Policy for Ekthaa Business

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Ekthaa Business ("Ekthaa", "we", "our", or "us"). We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Ekthaa Business mobile application (the "App") and related services.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal & Business Information

Information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Business name, address, business type
  • GST number (if provided)
  • Business registration details
  • Owner or representative name
  • Phone number and email address
  • Login credentials (stored securely)

1.2 Customer Data (Entered by Business Users)

When you use Ekthaa Business to manage customers, you may store:

  • Customer names and contact details
  • Credit and payment records
  • Transaction history
  • Purchase details
  • Outstanding balances
Note: You are responsible for ensuring you have lawful permission to store your customers' data.

1.3 Product & Inventory Information

  • Product or service names
  • Descriptions and categories
  • Prices and stock levels
  • Product images uploaded by you

1.4 Location Information

We may collect location data to:

  • Display your business on maps
  • Enable local discovery features
  • Improve accuracy of nearby searches

You can disable location access anytime through your device settings.

1.5 Camera Permission

We request camera permission to enable you to:

  • Take photos of products for your inventory
  • Scan QR codes for quick customer identification
  • Capture business-related images

Camera access is only used when you explicitly choose to take a photo within the app. We do not access your camera in the background.

1.6 Transaction Information

  • Invoice records
  • Payment dates and amounts
  • Transaction history
  • Payment methods (if applicable)

1.7 Device & Technical Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and app version
  • Device identifiers
  • Network and diagnostic information
